RIM Calculation Hints:
• Residual Income (RI): Calculated as EPS - (Opening BVPS × Cost of Equity).
• Capital Charge: The minimum return to investors require based on the risk (Ke).
• Terminal Value: Calculated on using (Gordon Growth formula) on the Last year's Residual Income: RI_final × (1+g) / (Ke - g).
• Intrinsic Value: The sum of Current BVPS + Sum of PV of all future Residual Incomes.
